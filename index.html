Backpack Contents

There is pure delight in pursuing a walk along the length of the Ganges River. Flowing 2,500 kilometers from its source in the Himalayan Mountains to the Bay of Bengal.

Of particular enjoyment, is meeting the beautiful people who live along the rivers banks. They are some of the most hospitable people on earth.

To enjoy the journey to the fullest, I have chosen to conduct the walk with a minimum of baggage. Here is my complete list.

  • simple backpack
  • simple bedsheet
  • one change of clothing
  • torch
  • odomous
  • water bottle
  • water treatment
  • band aids
  • headgear
  • sunscreen
  • mobile phone
  • mobile charger or battery pack
Footwear

Sandals are my footwear of choice for this walk. Others may find an approach shoe more comfortable. No matter what footwear you choose, the rule of thumb is that they should be well used before embarking on this journey. Too many of my companions have turned up with a fairly new shoe and paid the price in blisters and sores.
